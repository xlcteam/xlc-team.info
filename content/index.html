Hi There!

We are the XLC Team, a robotic team from Slovakia.

Most of the time we build robots.
Some of them play soccer.
Some are trying to act as rescuers.
Some even dance.
Nearly all of them participate in the RoboCup Junior competition.

We also teach others how to build and program them.

Apart from that we build software which makes our life easier.
It might also help you so be sure to check it out

We do all of this because we can.
And we can because we are driven by passion.
And supported by our sponsors.
And we are thankful for any support.

And we love to speak about what we do or just having an offtopic conversation.
So don't hesitate to contact us.
