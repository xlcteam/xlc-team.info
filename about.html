The Name

There is a nice story about our name. In our first years we were proudly using the pseudo-crypthographic name 0x584c43. It’s the hex representation of the string XLC. And how did we came up with that? Well, we were always fans of doing things simply. Sometimes extremely simply. And so we thought that is might be a good idea to express this in our name too.

We had a little discussion and came up with the name XLC which can be an abbreviation of

eXtremely Lite Construction or

eXtremely Lite Code

which shows the two crucial parts of every robot: hardware and software.
